distinct

adj
英音 [dɪ'stɪŋkt]
美音 [dɪ'stɪŋkt]
词频 2书面 1阅读 1

英文释义

  • ★ 明显不同的；截然不同的 (clearly different or of a different kind：)

    • In Beijing, the four seasons are all very distinct.

      北京四季分明。

    • The African elephant is divided into two distinct species.

      非洲象分为两个不同的种类。

  • 明显的；清楚的 (easily or clearly seen, heard, smelled, etc.：)

    • distinct writing

      清晰的笔迹
