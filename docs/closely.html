closely

adv

英文释义

  • ★ 接近地；仔细地；严密地：

    • The students were sitting closely together and whispering.

      学生们紧挨着坐在一起窃窃私语。

    • Watch the pets closely when they are left outdoors.

      把宠物放在室外时要仔细照看他们。
