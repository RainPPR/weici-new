judgment

n
英音 ['dʒʌdʒmənt]
美音 ['dʒʌdʒmənt]
词频 3书面 2

英文释义

  • ★ 判断 (力)；辨别力 (the ability to form opinions or to make sensible decisions：)

    • He showed excellent judgement in choosing a friend.

      他在择友方面表现出了敏锐的判断力。

  • ★ 意见；看法；评价 (an opinion that you form after thinking carefully：)

    • make a judgement on / about

      对…作出评价

    • In her judgment, it was a wrong thing to give children so much pocket money.

      在她看来，给孩子们这么多零花钱是不对的。

  • 审判
