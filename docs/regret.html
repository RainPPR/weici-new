regret

vt 重难点
英音 [rɪ'ɡret]
美音 [rɪ'ɡret]
词频 4书面 1

用法点拨

(regretted, regretted)

英文释义

  • ★ 后悔；懊悔 (to feel sorry about sth you have done ; to wish that you had not done sth：)

    • He who will not learn when he is young will regret it when he is old.

      少壮不努力，老大徒伤悲。

  • ★ [正式用语] 抱歉；遗憾 (used to say in a formal way that you are sorry about a situation：)

    • It is much to be regretted that you can’t join us in the trip.

      你不能和我们一道去旅行，真是遗憾。

      非常遗憾，我不能接受你的盛情邀请。

      接受你的辞呈，我感到非常遗憾。
