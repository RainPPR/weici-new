expect

vt 重难点
英音 [ɪk'spekt]
美音 [ɪk'spekt]
词频 5口语 3书面 3

英文释义

  • ★ 等待 (to be waiting for sb / sth to arrive：)

    • expect a letter / phone call

      等信/电话

    • Are you expecting visitors?

      你在等客人吗？

  • ★ 预料；预计 (to think or believe that sth will happen or sb will do sth：)

    • The plan goes much as expected.

      计划进展得如预期一样。

  • ★ 要求；期望；指望 (to demand that sb will do sth because it is their duty：)
    • ★ 料想；认为：

      • I expect you are right.

        我想你是对的。

      • — Will he be late?

        他会迟到吗？

      • — I expect so / not.

        我想会的/不会。

    固定搭配

    be expecting a baby / child

    怀孕：

    • If you are expecting a baby, your smoking habit might cause problems for it.

      如果你怀孕了，吸烟的习惯可能会对胎儿产生不良影响。
