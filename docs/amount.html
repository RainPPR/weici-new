amount to:

达到 (某数量)；合计：

  • At the start of this year, foreigners living in Italy amounted to 4.56 million.

    今年年初，居住在意大利的外国人数量达到了456万。

相当于；等于；意味着：

  • Keeping what belongs to another amounts to stealing.

    占有别人的东西就相当于偷窃。
