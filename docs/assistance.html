英文释义

  • ★ 帮助；协助；援助：

    • medical / technical assistance

      医疗援助/技术支持

    • give / offer / provide assistance to sb

      帮助某人

    • With the assistance of his brother, he sold one painting.

      在弟弟的帮助下，他卖掉了一幅画。

    • Can we be of assistance (= can we help you) in any way?

      我们能帮上什么忙吗？
