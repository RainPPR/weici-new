agreement

n
英音 [ə'ɡriːmənt]
美音 [ə'ɡriːmənt]
词频 4口语 2书面 3

英文释义

  • ★ 同意；(意见) 一致
    反义词: disagreement：

    • It’s easier for two parties to reach agreement than for three.

      两方达成一致比三方达成一致要容易。

    • We are in agreement about the price.

      关于价格我们意见一致。

  • ★ 协定；协议：

    • sign an agreement

      签订协议

    • arrive at / come to / reach an agreement ( with)

      (与…)达成协议

    • hold to / keep an agreement

      遵守协议

    • They had an agreement never to talk about work at home.

      他们约定在家中绝不谈工作。
