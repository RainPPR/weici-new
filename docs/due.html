due

adj 重难点
英音 [djuː]
美音 [duː]
词频 4口语 3书面 3

英文释义

  • ★ 预定的；预期的 (arranged or expected to happen or arrive at a particular time：)

    • Please make sure you return the book before it’s due.

      请确保在到期前还书。

    • After only six months he was due for a raise if this assessment was satisfactory.

      如果这份评价令人满意，仅6个月后他就会得到加薪。

  • ★ (钱) 到期的：

    • The next income tax payment is due on January 31.

      下一次的所得税支付期限是1月31日。

  • 应得的；欠下的 (owed to sb either as a debt or because they have done sth to deserve it：)

    • Our thanks are due to the whole team.

      我们要向全队致谢。

    • The wages due to him will be paid tomorrow.

      他应得的工资明天付给他。

  • 适当的；合适的 (proper or suitable：)

    • No matter how ordinary a job is, it plays a part in society and therefore deserves our due respect.

      无论工作多么普通，都在社会中起着作用，因此值得我们尊敬。

固定搭配

due to

由于；因为：

  • The town was cut off from the rest of the world due to heavy snow.

    由于暴雪，这个小镇与外界隔绝了。

  • The water shortage is severe mainly due to global warming, environmental pollution and the everincreasing population.

    水资源严重短缺主要是由全球变暖、环境污染和人口持续增长造成的。
