interrupt

v
英音 [ˌɪntə'rʌpt]
美音 [ˌɪntə'rʌpt]
词频 3

英文释义

  • ★ 打断；打扰：

    • Don't interrupt the speaker; ask your questions afterwards.

      不要打断演讲者，等他讲完你再提问。

    • I'm sorry to interrupt, but there's something important I have to tell you.

      抱歉打扰一下，我有要事相告。

  • ★ 中断；阻断：

    • Traffic was interrupted by floods.

      交通被洪水阻断。
