vt

美音 [ə'ses]
词频 3口语 2书面 2阅读 2

英文释义

  • ★ 评价；评定 (to make a judgment about a person or situation(judge)：)

    • It’s difficult to assess the impact of the President’s speech.

      总统讲话产生的影响很难估计。

    • Many of the adults were assessed as having learning difficulties.

      许多成年人被认定有学习困难。

  • ★ 估算；估价 (to calculate the value or cost of sth (estimate)：)

    • Damage to the building was assessed at £40 000.

      该建筑物的损失估定为4万英镑。
