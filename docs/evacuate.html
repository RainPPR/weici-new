evacuate

v
英音 [ɪ'vækjueɪt]
美音 [ɪ'vækjueɪt]
词频 1

英文释义

  • 疏散；撤出

    • Children were evacuated from London to escape the bombing.

      为躲避轰炸，孩子们都撤离了伦敦。

    • During the war he was evacuated to Scotland.

      战争期间他被撤离到苏格兰。

  • 撤离

    • Employees were urged to evacuate their offices immediately.

      已敦促各雇员立即从办公室撤出。

    • Locals were told to evacuate.

      当地居民已收到撤离的通知。
