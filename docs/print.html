英文释义

  • ★ 打印；印刷：

    • print the document

      打印文件

    • Six thousand copies of the book are printed.

      这本书印了6 000册。

  • ★ 刊登 (publish)：

    • It is his wish that his stories would be printed in local newspapers.

      他希望自己的故事会刊登在当地报纸上。

  • ★ 印 (照片)：

    • It usually takes a couple of hours for the pictures to be developed and printed.

      冲印照片通常需要几个小时。

    你可以使用网络打印机把数据打印出来。
