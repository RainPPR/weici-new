英文释义

  • ★ [C] 优先事项 (sth that is more important or that you must do before anything else：)

    • Education is a top priority.

      教育是当务之急。

    • Nowadays the priority for travelling is shifted from shopping to food and scenery.

      如今，人们旅游时优先考虑的事情从购物转移到美食和风景。

  • ★ [U] 优先权：

    • Club members will be given priority.

      俱乐部成员享有优先权。
