  • ★ [U] 能力 (the fact of being able to do sth：)
    反义词: inability
    • ★ [C] & [U] 才能；才识；技能 (a level of skill at doing sth：)

      • develop an ability

        培养技能

      • musical / natural ability

        音乐才能/天赋

      • Students of all abilities will get together and study for two weeks.

        各种水平的学生将聚集在一起学习两个星期。

    固定搭配

    to the best of one's ability

    尽最大努力：

    • I try to do my job to the best of my ability.

      我尽全力做好我的工作。
