appeal

v 重难点
英音 [ə'piːl]
美音 [ə'piːl]
词频 3口语 1书面 1阅读 2

英文释义

  • ★ 呼吁；恳请 (to make a serious request for sth you need or want very much：)
    • ★ 有吸引力；有感染力 (to be attractive or interesting to sb：)
      • 上诉；申诉：

        • She’s not happy with the decision and plans to appeal.

          她不服判决，打算上诉。

        • He appealed the case five times, always losing.

          这个案件他上诉了五次，都败诉了。

      • 启发；激发 (to influence sb’s feelings or thoughts so that they will do sth you want：)
