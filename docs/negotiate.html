英文释义

  • ★ 谈判；协商 (to try to reach an agreement by formal discussion, especially in business or politics：)

    • The government refused to negotiate with terrorists.

      政府拒绝与恐怖分子谈判。

    • We have been negotiating for more pay.

      我们一直在为增加工资进行协商。

  • ★ vt 商定；达成 (协议)：

    • negotiate a deal / contract

      达成交易 / 确立合同
