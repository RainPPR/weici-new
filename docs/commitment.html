commitment

n
英音 [kə'mɪtmənt]
美音 [kə'mɪtmənt]
词频 2口语 2书面 2阅读 2

英文释义

  • ★ [U] 投入；奉献 (being prepared to give a lot of your time and attention to sth because you believe it is right or important：)

    • A career as an actor requires one hundred percent commitment.

      演员这一行需要百分之百的投入。

  • ★ [C] 承诺；许诺；保证 (a promise or agreement to do sth; a responsibility：)

    • She has made a commitment to going bottle free.

      她承诺不饮用瓶装水。

  • ★ [C] 承诺的事 / 责任：

    • I’m overworked at the moment — I’ve taken on too many commitments.

      我目前劳累过度，承担的事情太多了。
