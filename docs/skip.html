skip

v
英音 [skɪp]
美音 [skɪp]
词频 3

英文释义

  • 跳；跳过：

    • skip over the fence

      跳过栅栏

    • She watched her little granddaughter skip down the path.

      她看着小孙女沿着小路边走边跳。

  • 略过；遗漏 (to miss the next thing that you would normally read, do, etc.：)

    • I skipped over the last part of the book.

      我略过了那本书的最后一部分。

  • [非正式用语]不做 (应做的事等)： (to not do sth that you usually do or should do (miss)：)

    • If you skip meals, you don’t get enough calories, and then you feel tired.

      如果不吃饭，身体就不会获得足够的热量，你就会感到疲劳。

    • She decided to skip the afternoon’s class.

      她决定下午的课不去上了。

固定搭配

skip a grade
