英文释义

  • ★ 踢；踢腿：

    • a free / penalty kick

      任意球/点球

  • [非正式用语] 极度的兴奋；极大的乐趣 (a feeling of great pleasure, excitement, etc.：)

    • It gave the children a kick to see their own performance on TV.

      看到自己在电视上的表演让孩子们极其兴奋。

    • The kid will get a kick out of finding the answer by himself.

      孩子自己找到答案会极其兴奋。
