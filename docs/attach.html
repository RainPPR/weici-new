attach

v 重难点
英音 [ə'tætʃ]
美音 [ə'tætʃ]
词频 3口语 2书面 2阅读 3

英文释义

  • ★ 系；贴；连接 (to fasten or join one thing to another：)

    • I attached a photo to my application form.

      我在申请表上贴了一张照片。

    • At the station, a board went up with dollar bills attached to it with pins.

      车站竖起一个牌子，上面用别针别着些美元钞票。

  • [正式用语] (与…) 有关联：

    • the advantage that attaches to wealth

      与财富有关的有利条件

  • ★ 重视；认为...重要 (to believe that sth is important or worth thinking about：)

    • To be honest, a lot of people attach great importance to becoming rich and famous.

      坦白地说，很多人把名声和财富看得很重。

    • Learning strategies, to which teachers attach importance / significance, haven’t drawn enough attention of students.

      老师重视的学习策略还没有引起学生们足够的注意。
