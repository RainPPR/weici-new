bother

v
英音 ['bɒðə(r)]
美音 ['bɑːðər]
词频 3口语 3书面 1

英文释义

  • ★ [常用于否定句和疑问句]费心 ；麻烦 (to spend time and / or energy doing sth：)

    • He didn’t bother to answer the question.

      他懒得回答这个问题。

    • — I’ll go to the post office to mail this parcel to Ann.

      我要去邮局给安寄这个包裹。

    • — Why bother? I’ll see her at dinner this evening.

      不必麻烦了吧？今晚吃饭我能见到她。

  • ★ 使烦恼；使苦恼 (worry)；使疼痛 (to annoy, worry or upset sb; to cause sb trouble or pain:)

    • You needn’t bother yourself about / with small things.

      你用不着为琐事烦恼。

    • My wrist has been bothering me.

      我手腕一直痛。

  • ★ 打扰 ( to interrupt sb (trouble)：)

    • Don’t bother me while I’m reading.

      我看书的时候别打扰我。

    • Sorry to bother you, but the manager is on the line.

      对不起，打扰一下，经理打电话找你。
