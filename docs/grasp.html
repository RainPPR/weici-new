grasp

vt
英音 [ɡrɑːsp]
美音 [ɡræsp]
词频 3

英文释义

  • ★ 抓住；紧握 (to take a firm hold of sb / sth：)

    • Grasp all, lose all.

      贪多必失。

    • He grasped my hand and shook it warmly.

      他热情地抓着我的手握了起来。

    • It's our hope that you will eagerly grasp every opportunity to do well for yourself and others.

      我们希望你无论为自己还是为他人，都要热切地抓住每一次机会把事情做好。

    • We should grasp the opportunities of the nation's new round of opening-up.

      我们应该抓住国家新一轮的对外开放的机会。

  • ★ 理解；领会 (to understand sth completely：)

    • grasp sb's meaning

      理解某人的意思

    • At that time, we did not fully grasp the significance of what had happened.

      当时，我们没有完全明白所发生之事的重要性。
