refer

v 重难点
英音 [rɪ'fɜː(r)]
美音 [rɪ'fɜːr]
词频 5口语 3书面 3

固定搭配

refer (...) to

谈到；提到 (to mention or speak about sb / sth：)

  • The scientist referred to global warming at least three times in his speech.

    那位科学家在演讲中至少3次提到了全球气候变暖。

  • The hospital now refers to patients by name, not case number.

    医院现在叫病人的名字，不叫病例号码了。

查阅；参考 (to look at sth or ask sb for information (consult)：)

  • refer to a dictionary to check the spelling

    查一下词典看看拼写是否正确

  • While giving the English speech, one is not allowed to refer to the notes.

    作英语演讲时，参赛选手不得看讲稿。

指的是 (to describe or be connected to sb / sth：)

  • Pritzker Architecture Prize is <b>referred to</b> as the Nobel Prize in architecture.

    普利兹克建筑奖有建筑界的诺贝尔奖之称。

把…交送给：

  • My colleague said that as the situation was unusual he would <b>refer</b> the matter <b>to</b> his editor.

    我同事说由于情况非同寻常，他会把这个问题交给他的编辑处理。
