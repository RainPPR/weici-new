固定搭配

consist in

在于；存在于：

  • Happiness consists in contentment.

    知足常乐。

  • Success consists in the ability to continue efforts through failures.

    成功在于百折不挠。

consist of

由…构成；由…组成：

  • How many countries does the UK consist of?

    大不列颠联合王国由多少个国家组成？

  • Life consists of not only sunshine but also storms.

    生活不仅有阳光，还有风雨。
