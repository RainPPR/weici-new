achieve

v 重难点
英音 [əˈtʃiːv]
美音 [əˈtʃiːv]
词频 4口语 2书面 3阅读 3

英文释义

  • ★ (凭借努力) 达到 (目标、地位、标准) (to succeed in reaching a particular goal, status or standard, especially after much effort (attain)：)

    • achieve success / progress

      取得成功 / 进步

    • Happiness is achieved through hard work.

      幸福是奋斗出来的。

    • I used to try effective methods to achieve my academic goals.

      为实现自己的学习目标，我曾尝试过一些有效的方法。

  • 完成 (to succeed in doing sth or causing sth to happen (accomplish)：)

    • No one can achieve anything without effort.

      没有人能够不努力就有所成就。

    • He achieved what he had thought impossible.

      他做成了自己以为不可能的事。

  • 获得成功 (to be successful：)

    • He is an excellent lawyer with a good brain and a determination to achieve.

      他是位优秀的律师，具有好的头脑和取得成功的决心。
