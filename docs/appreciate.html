英文释义

  • ★ 欣赏；赏识： (to understand how good or useful sb / sth is：)

    • I really appreciate your handwriting.

      你字写得真好。

    • His talents aren’t appreciated by his boss, which makes him depressed.

      他的才干不为老板赏识，这使得他很沮丧。

  • ★ 感激；感谢： (to be grateful for sth：)

    • I appreciate your help very much.

      非常感谢你的帮助。

  • 理解；明白： (to understand that sth is ture(realize)：)

    • appreciate the significance / value of sth

      理解某事的重要性/意义
