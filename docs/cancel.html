英文释义

  • ★ 取消 (计划好的事情)：

    • She cancel(l)ed her appointment with her friends.

      她取消了和朋友的约会。

  • 撤销；使作废 (to end an agreement or arrangement that you don’t want any more：)

    • Is it too late to cancel my order?

      我现在撤销订单是不是太晚了？
