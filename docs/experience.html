experience

n 重难点
英音 [ɪk'spɪəriəns]
美音 [ɪk'spɪriəns]
词频 5口语 3书面 3

英文释义

  • ★ 经验；实践 (the knowledge and skill gained through time spent doing a job or activity：)

    • I've got a lot of experience in teaching English.

      我有丰富的英语教学经验。

    • I have no driving experience.

      我没有驾驶经验。

  • ★ 经历；阅历 (the knowledge you get from life and from being in a lot of different situations：)

    • From my personal experience, I know the traffic in Beijing is terrible.

      根据我个人的经历，北京的交通状况很糟糕。

  • ★ (一次) 经历 (sth that happens to you or sth you do：)

    • Travelling along the old Silk Road is an interesting and rewarding experience.

      沿着古老的丝绸之路旅行是一次有趣而又有益的经历。

    • I had many wonderful experiences, but I also had a sad one.

      我有很多美好的经历，但也有一次糟糕的。

    • He thought the trip was a life changing experience.

      他认为这次旅行是改变人生的一次经历。
