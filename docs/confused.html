英文释义

  • ★ 困惑的；糊涂的 (unable to understand or think clearly what sb is saying or what is happening：)

    • If you have questions or are confused about anything, email me.

      如果你有问题或是对什么事有疑问，发邮件给我。

  • ★ 混乱的；难懂的 (not clear or not easy to understand：)

    • The children gave a confused account of what had happened.

      孩子们把发生的事描述得颠三倒四。
