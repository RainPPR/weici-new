n

词频 2

英文释义

  • ★ [C] & [U] 黎明；拂晓：

    • from dawn till dusk

      从早到晚

    • The train left late that night and arrived in Montreal at dawn the next morning.

      火车深夜启程，第二天黎明时分到达了蒙特利尔。

    • We arrived in Sydney as dawn broke.

      天亮时，我们抵达悉尼。

  • 开端；起始：

    • The book is about the dawn of civilization.

      这本书讲述文明的起源。
