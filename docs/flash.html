    英文释义

    • ★ (使) 闪光；(使) 闪耀：

      • Lightning flashed in the sky.

        闪电在空中划过。

      • He flashed the light in my eyes.

        他用光晃我的眼。

      • Police cars arrived with their blue lights flashing.

        警车到了，蓝色的警灯闪烁着。

      • Why is that driver flashing his lights at / towards me?

        那个司机为什么朝我闪车灯呢？

    • 闪现；突然想到：

      • A smile flashed across Kathy's face.

        凯西的脸上闪过一丝微笑。

      • Memories flashed through his mind.

        回忆掠过他的脑海。

      • A good idea flashed into my mind.

        我突然想到一个好的主意。

      • Images of the war flashed across the screen.

        屏幕上闪过战争的画面。
