英文释义

  • ★ 看得见的 反【invisible】；明显的：

    • The fish are visible below the surface of the lake.

      鱼在湖面下清晰可见。

    • For several hours I made myself highly visible, making sure everyone could see me.

      我在非常显眼的地方站了几个小时，确保每个人都能看见我。
