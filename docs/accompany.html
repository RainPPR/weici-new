英文释义

  • ★ 陪伴；伴随：

    • Children under 14 must be accompanied by an adult.

      不满14岁的孩子必须有大人陪同。

    • Thunder usually accompanies lightning.

      雷声常常伴随着闪电。

  • 为...伴奏：

    • Mary sang and I accompanied her on the piano.

      玛丽唱歌，我弹钢琴伴奏。
