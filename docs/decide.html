decide

v
英音 [dɪ'saɪd]
美音 [dɪ'saɪd]
词频 5口语 3书面 3

英文释义

  • ★ 决定；选定：

    • It’s difficult to decide between the two.

      两者之间难以取舍。

    • It’s the people who decide the fate of mankind.

      决定人类命运的还是人类自身。

    • We decided against going on a holiday abroad for lack of money.

      因为缺钱，我们决定不去国外度假了。

固定搭配

decide on / upon

决定；选定：

  • Have you decided on a date for the wedding?

    你们定好婚礼日期了吗？
