moment

n 重难点
英音 ['məʊmənt]
美音 ['moʊmənt]
词频 5口语 3书面 3

用法点拨

[C]

英文释义

  • ★ 某一时刻 (a particular point in time：)

    • at this / that moment

      就在这/那时

    • from that moment on

      从那以后

    • The best moment for the football star was when he scored the winning goal.

      那个足球明星最精彩的一刻就是他射入致胜一球的时候。

  • ★ 片刻；瞬间 (a very short period of time：)

    • Could I use your computer for a few moments, please?

      我能用一会儿你的电脑吗？

  • ★ 时机 (a particular time for doing sth：)

    • I’m waiting for the right moment to tell him the bad news.

      我正在等待适当的时机告诉他这个坏消息。

固定搭配

(at) any moment (now)

马上；立刻：

  • Hurry up! He’ll be back any moment now.

    快点儿！他马上就会回来。

(at) the last moment

(在) 最后一刻：

  • They changed the plans at the last moment.

    他们在最后一刻改变了计划。

at the moment

此刻；目前：

  • I’m terribly busy at the moment.

    我目前忙得很。

for a moment

片刻；一会儿：

  • He was silent for a moment.

    他沉默了一会儿。

for the moment

暂时；目前 (for the present)：

  • I’m not very happy at work, but I’ll stay there for the moment.

    我工作得不太开心，但暂时还会留在那里。

in a moment

马上；立即：

  • I’ll be ready in a moment.

    我马上就准备好。

one moment … the next

一会儿…过一会儿：

  • One moment it was quiet and calm in the forest, the next, the air was charged with tension.

    一会儿森林里寂静无声，过一会儿，空气中弥漫着紧张的气氛。

the moment (that)

[引导时间状语从句，相当于连词as soon as] 一… (就…)：

  • Alisha fell asleep the moment she got into bed.

    艾丽莎一上床就睡着了。

  • Be careful! The machine starts the moment you press the button.

    小心！这台机器一按按钮就会启动。
