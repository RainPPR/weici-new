ruin

vt
英音 ['ruːɪn]
美音 ['ruːɪn]
词频 4口语 1

英文释义

  • ★ 毁灭；(完全地) 毁坏 (to spoil or destroy sth completely：)

    • Don’t let a small disagreement ruin your friendship.

      不要让小小的分歧破坏你们的友谊。

    • She returned home and found all her furniture had been ruined by the flood.

      她回家后发现家具全被洪水毁坏了。

  • ★ 使破产 (to make sb lose all his / her money：)

    • If she loses the court case it will ruin her.

      如果败诉，她就倾家荡产了。

ruin

n
英音 ['ruːɪn]
美音 ['ruːɪn]
词频 4

英文释义

  • ★ [U] 毁灭；破产：

    • the ruin of civilization

      文明的毁灭

    • financial ruin

      破产

    • fall in / into ruin

      灭亡；荒废

  • ★ [C] [常用复数] 废墟；遗迹；残余：

    • the ruins of Rome

      古罗马遗迹

固定搭配

in ruins

成废墟；严重受损：

  • In fifteen terrible seconds a large city <b>lay in ruins</b>.

    在可怕的15秒内，一座大城市就沦为一片废墟。
