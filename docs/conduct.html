conduct

vt 重难点
英音 [kən'dʌkt]
美音 [kən'dʌkt]
词频 4书面 2阅读 3

英文释义

  • 引导；带领： (to lead or guide sb through or around a place：)

    • The guide conducted us around the West Lake.

      导游带领我们游览了西湖。

  • ★ 指挥 (军队、乐队等)：

    • My friend went home, leaving me to conduct the band.

      我朋友回家了，留下我指挥乐队。

  • ★ 实施；管理；进行 (to organize or do a particular activity：)

    • conduct an experiment / an interview / a survey

      进行实验/面试/调查

    • A lot of research is being conducted in this area.

      很多研究工作正在这个领域内展开。

    • So far we have conducted a variety of activities on English learning.

      目前我们已经开展了各种各样的英语学习活动。

  • 举止；表现：

    • Make sure you conduct yourself as a good neighbor should.

      确保你的行为举止是一个好邻居应该有的。
