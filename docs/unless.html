英文释义

  • ★ 除非；如果不(if ... not)：

    • Unless the weather gets better, I’ll stay at home.

      如果天气没有好转，我就待在家里。

    • He told me that I’d probably have a heart attack unless I started eating less.

      他告诉我，如果我再不减少食量，就有可能患心脏病。
