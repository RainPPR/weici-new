deliver

vt
英音 [dɪ'lɪvə(r)]
美音 [dɪ'lɪvər]
词频 4口语 2书面 2阅读 3

英文释义

  • ★ vt & vi 递送；传送 ( to take goods, letters, packages, etc. to a particular place or person：)

    • deliver a letter / message

      送信/带信；传话

    • I usually have fast food delivered to my office.

      我通常让（人）把快餐送到我的办公室。

    • Your food will be delivered in half an hour. Thanks for calling.

      您的订餐将于半小时后送达，感谢您的来电。

    • We promise to deliver free of charge within 48 hours.

      我们承诺在48小时内免费送达。

  • ★ 发表；表达；讲 (to give a speech, talk, etc. or other official statement：)

    • deliver a speech / lecture

      作报告/授课

  • 接生；使分娩：

    • Her husband had to deliver the baby himself.

      她丈夫不得不亲自接生。
