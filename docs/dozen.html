dozen

n
英音 ['dʌzn]
美音 ['dʌzn]
词频 4口语 2书面 1

用法点拨

(pl dozen / dozens) [C]

英文释义

  • ★ (一)打；十二个；十几个；几十；许多：

    • two dozen bottles of orange

      两打瓶装橙汁

    • More than a dozen students in that school were sent abroad to study medicine last year.

      去年，那所学校派了十几个学生到国外学医。

固定搭配

dozens of

很多：

  • The hurricane blew away dozens of farming villages and brought down thousands of trees.

    飓风刮走了很多村庄，刮倒了数千棵树。
