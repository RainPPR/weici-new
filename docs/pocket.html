  • ★ 衣袋；口袋；钱财：

    • Better sense in the head than cents in the pocket.

      她的钱包在火车上被偷了。

      袖珍字典

    • pocket money

      零用钱

    • The organization has very deep pockets (= a lot of money).

      这家机构资金雄厚。
