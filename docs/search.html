英文释义

  • ★ (在...中) 搜寻 (to look carefully for sth / sb：)
    • ★ (对某人) 搜身 ((especially of the police) to carefully examine sth / sb for sth that is hidden：)
      • ★ 探索；思索 (to try to find an answer to a problem：)

        • She paused, searching for inspiration.

          她停了下来寻找灵感。

        • The general manager promised to search into the matter.

          总经理答应深入调查此事。
