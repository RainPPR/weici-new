wash

英文释义

  • ★ 洗 (手、脸、身体等)；洗涤：

    • wash clothes / dishes

      洗衣服/碗

    • Wash your hands before eating.

      吃东西前要先洗手。

    • My husband washes and irons every day.

      我丈夫每天又洗又熨。

  • 耐洗：

    • Does this dress wash well?

      这件连衣裙耐洗吗？

  • 冲走；冲出：

    • Soil has been washed into the river from the riverbank or from the nearby fields.

      泥土从河岸或者附近的田地被冲进河里。

固定搭配

wash away

冲掉；洗掉；消除…的影响：

  • The flood <b>washed</b> some of the houses <b>away</b> in the town.

    洪水冲走了镇上的一些房屋。
