fortune

n
英音 ['fɔːtʃuːn]
美音 ['fɔːrtʃən]
词频 4口语 1书面 1

英文释义

  • ★ 机遇；运气 (chance or luck, especially in the way it affects people's lives：)

    • If you don't work hard, no fortune will come.

      如果你不努力，机遇是不会来的。

    • The Chinese dragon is a bringer of good fortune.

      “中国龙”会给人带来好运。

    • In China, children are given money in a red envelope to bring good fortune in the New Year.

      在中国，人们会在新年时把钱装在红包里给孩子们，以求带来好运。

  • 时运；命运：

    • tell sb's fortune

      给某人算命

    • What will be our fortune?

      我们的命运将如何？

  • ★ (大量) 财产；大笔的钱： (a large amount of money：)

    • make a fortune

      发财；发迹

    • a small fortune (= a quite large fortune)

      一大笔钱

    • He received a large fortune when his uncle died.

      他叔父去世时，他得到一大笔财产。

    • The fortune sets him free from financial worries.

      这笔钱使他不用为经济问题担忧。
