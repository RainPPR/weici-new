practice

n 重难点
英音 ['præktɪs]
美音 ['præktɪs]
词频 5口语 2书面 3

英文释义

  • ★ (反复) 训练；练习：

    • Practice makes perfect.

      熟能生巧。

    • John is at baseball practice.

      约翰正在进行棒球训练。

    • To learn English well requires a lot of practice.

      学好英语需要大量的练习。

  • ★ 实践；实际行动：

    • We must combine theory with practice.

      我们必须把理论与实践相结合。

  • ★ 惯例；习惯做法；习俗 (the usual or expected way of doing sth; a habit or custom：)

    • according to the international practice

      按照国际惯例

    • The practice of students endlessly copying letters and sentences from a blackboard is a thing of the past.

      学生们没完没了地从黑板上抄写单词和句子的做法是过去的事了。

    • Customs are common social practices.

      习俗指的是普遍的社会惯常做法。

固定搭配

in practice

实际上；事实上：

  • Your suggestion is very good in theory, but in practice it will not work.

    理论上，你的建议很好，但实际上，是行不通的。

out of practice

生疏；疏于练习：

  • If you don’t play, you’ll get out of practice.

    不练习就会生疏。

put ... into practice

将…付诸实践：

  • She’s been able to put a lot of what she’s learnt into practice in her home.

    她在自己家里已经能实践很多自己学到的东西。
