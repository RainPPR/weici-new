  • ★ 问；询问：

    • She asked Mary what time it was.

      她问玛丽几点了。

    • We’ll have to ask someone the way to the station.

      我们得去问一下车站怎么走。

    • She asked about Jesse’s health.

      她询问了杰西的健康状况。
