activity

n
英音 [æk'tɪvəti]
美音 [ækˈtɪvəti]
词频 5口语 2书面 3

英文释义

  • ★ [C] [常用单数] (出于爱好的) 活动 (a thing that you do for interest or pleasure：)

    • outdoor activities

      户外活动

    • Sailing is an activity I much enjoy.

      帆船运动是我非常喜爱的活动。

    • After-class activities are treated as an essential part of school life.

      课外活动被视为学校生活不可或缺的一部分。

  • ★ [C] & [U] (有组织的，有目的的) 活动 (a thing that you do in order to achieve a particular aim：)

    • fund-raising activities

      筹款活动

  • ★ [U] (正在进行的或正在做的) 活动 (a situation in which sth is happening or a lot of things are happening or being done：)

    • The streets were noisy and full of activity.

      街道上一片喧嚣与忙碌。

    • Regular physical activity helps to control your weight.

      经常活动身体有助于控制体重。
