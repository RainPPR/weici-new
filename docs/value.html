value

n
英音 ['væljuː]
美音 ['væljuː]
词频 5口语 2书面 3

英文释义

  • ★ [C] & [U] (金钱方面的) 价值；价格 (the amount of money that sth is worth：)

    • As a big-time bargain hunter, I know the value of a dollar.

      作为一位顶级的淘便宜货的人，我知道1美元的价值。

    • market value

      市场价格

  • ★ [C] & [U] 等值；划算程度 (how much sth is worth compared with its price：)

    • be good / bad value (= worth / not worth the money it costs)

      很划算/不值

    • Every customer is looking for value for money (= good value).

      每位顾客都想买到划算的东西。

  • ★ [U] 重要性；用处；益处 (the importance of sth (benefit)：)

    • Most people know the value of a good education.

      大多数人都知道良好教育的重要性。

  • ★ [用复数] 价值观念；社会准则 (mor a l or professional standards of behaviour; principles：)

    • During the Renaissance, new ideas and values gradually replaced those held in the Middle Ages.

      文艺复兴时期，新思想和新观念逐渐取代了中世纪的思想和观念。

固定搭配

of value

有价值的；有用的 (valuable)：

  • Sports can be <b>of great value</b>, especially to people who work with their brains most of the day.

    体育运动很有益处，对那些每天大部分时间都在从事脑力劳动的人来说更是如此。
