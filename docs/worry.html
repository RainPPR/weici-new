worry

v
英音 ['wʌri]
美音 ['wɜːri]
词频 5口语 3书面 2

英文释义

  • ★ 使焦虑；使担忧 (to be anxious or unhappy about sb or sth：)

    • He worried that he would fail the exam.

      他担心会考试不及格。

    • Her child has a bad cough and it rather worries her.

      孩子咳嗽得很严重，令她十分担忧。

  • ★ 骚扰；烦扰；使不安 (bother)：

    • The noise never seems to worry her.

      这噪音似乎从未让她感到厌烦。

    • Don’t keep worrying him with a lot of silly questions.

      别老用这些愚蠢的问题烦他。

  • ★ 担心；发愁：

    • You have no cause / reason to worry.

      你没有理由担心。

    • Frank doesn’t worry about making mistakes.

      弗兰克不担心犯错。
