acknowledge

vt
英音 [əkˈnɒlɪdʒ]
美音 [əkˈnɑːlɪdʒ]
词频 2口语 1书面 1阅读 2

英文释义

  • ★ 承认 (to accept or admit that sth is true or exists：)

    • acknowledge one’s error

      承认错误

    • He is acknowledged as / to be one of the greatest pianists.

      他被认为是最杰出的钢琴家之一。

    • They acknowledged (to us) that they were defeated.

      他们(向我们)承认已被打败。

  • 向...打招呼；对...作出反应 (to show that you have seen or noticed sb / sth or received sth：)

    • I was standing right next to her, but she didn’t even acknowledge me.

      我就站在她旁边，可她连个招呼都不跟我打。

    • He acknowledged the greeting with a nod.

      他点头回应了问候。

  • (公开) 感谢 (to publicly express thanks for help you have been given：)

    • I should like to acknowledge all the help I have received from my family.

      我要感谢我的家人给予我的所有帮助。
