英文释义

  • ★ 把…联想 / 联系起来 (to connect sb or sth in your mind with sb or sth else：)

      我一想到瑞士就联想到阿尔卑斯山。

    • We tend to associate fresh air with health care.

      我们往往把新鲜空气和身体保健联系在一起。

  • 交往；结交 (to spend time with sb, especially sb that other people disapprove of：)

    • I don’t like you associating with such people.

      我不喜欢你和那些人混在一起。
