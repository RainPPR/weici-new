correspond

vi
英音 [ˌkɒrə'spɒnd]
美音 [ˌkɔːrə'spɑːnd]
词频 2

英文释义

  • ★ 相符合；相一致 (to be the same as or match (agree)：)

    • Your account of the events does not correspond with hers.

      你对事情的描述与她说的不相符。

    • Let your actions correspond to your words.

      你要言行一致。

  • 相当；相类似 (to be similar to：)

    • The American Congress corresponds to the British Parliament.

      美国国会相当于英国议会。

  • ★ [正式用语]通信：

    • She corresponds regularly with her former teacher.

      她和以前的老师定期通信。
