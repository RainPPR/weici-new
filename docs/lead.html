lead

v 重难点
英音 [liːd]
美音 [liːd]
词频 5口语 3书面 3

用法点拨

(led, led)

英文释义

  • ★ 引路 (guide)；通向：

    • Permit me to lead the way, sir.

      先生，请让我来带路吧。

    • This path will led you to the station.

      你走这条路可以到火车站。

    • a path leading up the hill

      通往山上的路

  • ★ 引领；率领 (to be in control or the leader in an organization, etc.：)

    • The captain led his team to victory.

      队长带领他的团队取得了胜利。

    • Some people say that she is too old to lead the country.

      有人说她年纪太大领导不了国家。

  • ★ 领先；率先 (to be winning or in first place in front of sb：)

    • He leads his class in English.

      他的英语是全班第一。

    • The country is leading in exporting cars.

      该国在汽车出口方面一直走在前列。

  • ★ 致使；导致 (to cause sth to happen or cause sb to do sth：)

    • Pain is your body telling you something's wrong, and continuing to exercise could lead to serious injury.

      疼痛是你的身体在告诉你出问题了，继续锻炼可能会导致严重的损伤。

    • A lack of confidence can lead to a lot of suffering.

      缺乏自信会导致很多痛苦。

    • Good habits always lead to high efficiency, while bad ones bring the opposite.

      好习惯总是会带来高效率，而坏习惯则相反。

  • ★ 过 (…的生活)：

    • lead a happy / hard / comfortable / simple life

      过着幸福的/艰苦的/舒适的/简朴的生活

固定搭配

lead up to

是…的先导；是导致…的原因：

  • the events leading up to the strike

    导致罢工的事件
