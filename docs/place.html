place

n
英音 [pleɪs]
美音 [pleɪs]
词频 5口语 3书面 3

用法点拨

[C]

英文释义

  • ★ 地方；地点；部位：

    • Birds seek safe places before a storm.

      鸟儿会在暴风雨来临前寻找安全的地方。

    • a sore place on the leg

      腿上的疼痛处

  • ★ 住所；场所：

    • Some of my friends are coming for dinner at my place.

      有几个朋友要来我家吃饭。

  • 位置；地位；身份：

    • Working has a very important place in all our lives.

      工作在我们生活中占据着重要位置。

  • 资格；名额；机会：

    • get a place in a law firm

      在律师事务所工作

    • They might just have a place left on the writing course.

      写作课或许正好还有一个剩余名额。

  • ★ 名次：

    • Luck was smiling on me that day as I won first place in the competition.

      那天幸运女神向我微笑，我获得了比赛第一名。

固定搭配

in place

在适当的位置；适当的；准备妥当：

  • The ropes hold you in place so that you don’t fall.

    绳子会把你固定在适当的位置，这样你就不会掉下去。

  • After the dinner, an expression of thanks to the host would be in place.

    饭后向主人道声谢是比较合适的。

  • The service could be in place by the end of the year.

    这项服务将于年底准备就绪。

in place of

代替；取代 (in sb’s / sth’s place)：

  • You can use yoghurt in place of cream to make this salad.

    你可以用酸奶替代奶油来做这道沙拉。

  • If I refused to go, they would send someone else in my place.

    如果我不去，他们会派其他人代替我的。

out of place

不在合适的位置上；不合适的；不相称的：

  • Some of these files seem to be out of place.

    有些文件似乎没放对地方。

  • I felt completely out of place among all these successful people.

    夹在这些成功人士中间，我觉得自己格格不入。

take place

发生；举行：

  • Great changes have taken place in the way people communicate in recent years.

    近年来，人们交流的方式发生了巨大的变化。

take the place of

代替 (take sb’s / sth’s place):

  • Computers have taken the place of typewriters in offices.

    在办公室里，电脑已取代了打字机。

  • She couldn’t attend the meeting so her assistant took her place.

    她不能出席会议，所以她的助手代替了她。

vt

英文释义

  • ★ 放置；安置：

    • He placed his fingers on the piano keys.

      他把手指放在钢琴键上。

  • ★ 发出 (订单)；登 (广告)：

    • place an order / advertisement

      下订单/登广告

  • 定名次：

    • He was placed third in last Saturday’s race.

      在上周六的比赛中，他名列第三。
