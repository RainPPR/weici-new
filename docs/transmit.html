英文释义

  • ★ 输送；传送；发射；播送：

    • Iron transmits heat.

      铁能导热。

    • Language, in its broad sense, is a way information is transmitted.

      从广义上说，语言是一种传递信息的方法。

    • The ceremony was transmitted live by satellite to over fifty countries.

      典礼通过卫星向五十多个国家实况转播。

  • 传播；传染：

    • This infection is transmitted to humans by mosquitoes.

      这种传染病是由蚊子传染给人类的。
