英文释义

  • ★ 犯 (错误、罪行) (to do sth wrong or illegal：)

    • commit murder / a crime / a mistake

      杀人/犯罪/犯错

    • Smoking is committing suicide slowly.

      吸烟等于慢性自杀。

  • ★ (使) 承担义务；(使) 作出保证 (to make a definite agreement or promise to do sth：)

    • The world has already committed to establishing a Climate Adaptation Fund.

      世界各国已承诺建立气候应对基金。

  • 投入 (to spend money or time on sth / sb：)
