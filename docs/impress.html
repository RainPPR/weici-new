impress

vt 重难点
英音 [ɪm'pres]
美音 [ɪm'pres]
词频 4口语 1书面 1

英文释义

  • 印；盖 (印) 于
    • ★ 给...留下极深的印象；使钦佩： (to make sb feel admiration and respect)

      • It impressed me that he remembered my name.

        让我印象深刻的是他记得我的名字。

    • [正式用语] 使意识到 (to try to make sb understand how important sth is：)

      • impress sth on sb's mind

        让某人牢记某事

      • The teacher impressed the importance of English on / upon me.

        老师让我意识到英语的重要性。
