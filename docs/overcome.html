(overcame, overcome)

英文释义

  • ★ 战胜；克服：

    • overcome the enemy / the difficulties

      战胜敌人/克服困难

    • If you work hard, you will overcome this problem.

      如果你努力的话，就会克服这个困难。

  • ★ [常用于被动语态] 受到…的极大影响：

    • Overcome by curiosity, the boy looked out the window.

      受好奇心驱使，小孩向窗外望去。

    • Her parents were overcome with great sadness at the funeral.

      在葬礼上她的父母悲痛欲绝。
